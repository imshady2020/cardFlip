About Card Flip

Card Flip pulls data from recently sold eBay listings and averages them together to give you a good estimate of what your sports cards are valued at. This is the first iteration of Card Flip, we are hoping to bring you more features in the future including:

  1. The ability to save cards to a wish list.
  2. Creating a collection or portfolio to track overall value.
  3. Creating alerts to be notified if certain price levels are reached.

Card Flip was built by Dom Vacchiano. It was built using Django, Bulma, and Python.
